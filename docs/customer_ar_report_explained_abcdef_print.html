APrinted title strip

Bold banner line (AUTOWAGEN — Accounts receivable) and As at DATE (Johannesburg). Same block appears first when you click Print / PDF on the live screen — it is formatted for filing or emailing as PDF.

BGrey help panels (three boxes)

  • Credit notes: how Balance is calculated with finalized credit notes (invoice total − payments − all finalized credits) and why AR cr. vs Refund columns exist.
  • Payable ≠ receivable: directs staff to supplier payable if they confuse the reports.
  • Overdue rule: ties to invoice due date vs report As at.

CFilters and buttons

  • As at — date printed on PDF; overdue math compares invoice due dates to this day.
  • Overdue only — narrows both tables to overdue-related rows (per app rules).
  • Account customers only — appears after sql/06a_customer_account.sql; limits to flagged account buyers.
  • Apply — reloads report with ticks/date.
  • Print / PDF — browser print; choose Save as PDF.
  • Sales invoices — shortcut to POS invoice list.

DTwo summary totals

  • Total owed (black card) — sum of customer balances in the filtered By customer list.
  • Of which overdue — portion of debt where due date < As at and balance > 0.

EBy customer table

  • One row per customer with unpaid final invoice balance.
  • Balance — net still owed (all credit types subtract from headline net due).
  • AR cr. / Refund — bookkeeping split of credit notes only (screens after Stage 7); both still reduced net due.
  • Overdue — that customer’s overdue portion only.
  • Statement — printable statement; Customer — opens customer edit.

FBy invoice (open balance) table

  • Each unfinished final sale with balance > 0.
  • Status — Overdue Nd (days), Open (has due date, not overdue yet), or No due date.
  • Open button on screen opens that invoice for payments / notes (hidden when printing).
  • Refund cn. — cash‑refund side of credits on that invoice (label may read Refund on customer row vs Refund cn. here).
Opposite report: money you owe suppliers = Reports → Accounts payable (owed). Guide with A–D: docs/supplier_ap_report_explained_print.html.
