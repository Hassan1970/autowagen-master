Covered in order: start a draft sale · pick or create a buyer · attach spare parts · (SA compliance reminder when selling used/stripped goods) · lock the invoice · print handout for the customer.
Note: Your shop decides who may finalize prices and refunds; screens match the shipped product (Stage 5 POS on invoice_edit.php).

Story you tell in a demo (short script)

  1. “Staff open POS → New sale; the system creates a private draft — nothing touches stock yet.”
  2. “They tie the invoice to whoever is paying: existing customer from the list, or New customer (quick) in two fields, then returning to finish the basket.”
  3. “Adding parts is easiest with Select item… search; power users can paste a numeric Part ID.”
  4. “When paperwork for second-hand buyer rules is required, our yellow reminder points to Customers — uploads happen once per buyer, reused on later sales.”
  5. “Finalize issues the numbered invoice and adjusts stock automatically; Print gives PDF or paper with your letterhead styling.”
